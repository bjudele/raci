Understanding RACI Roles

Responsible (R)

The person or role responsible for performing the task or decision. They are the doers.

Accountable (A)

The person or role ultimately accountable for the task or decision. They ensure that the task is completed.

Consulted (C)

The person or role who provides input or advice before the task is completed or the decision is made.

Informed (I)

The person or role who needs to be informed about the progress or outcome of the task or decision.
